# Subscription & Pricing

ExtraExtra offers three subscription tiers. All tiers include access to 100+ publishers, all 6 voice personalities, and all 3 listening modes.

## Pricing Tiers

### Free
**$0/month**

- Max podcast duration: 5 minutes
- Monthly listening limit: 30 minutes
- All publishers and voices included
- No Backstage access

Perfect for trying ExtraExtra and light news listeners.

---

### Pro
**$9.99/month**

- Max podcast duration: 10 minutes
- Monthly listening limit: 100 minutes
- All publishers and voices included
- Backstage access (see source articles)

Best for daily commuters and regular news consumers.

---

### Max
**$19.99/month**

- Max podcast duration: 20 minutes
- Monthly listening limit: 250 minutes
- All publishers and voices included
- Backstage access (see source articles)
- Priority generation queue (faster)

Best for power users and long commutes.

---

## Subscription Management

**Billing**
- Monthly billing through Apple App Store or Google Play Store
- Manage subscription in your device's app store settings
- Cancel anytime with no fees

**Upgrade/Downgrade**
- Upgrades take effect immediately
- Downgrades start at next billing cycle
- Changes managed through app store

## FAQ

**Can I use my subscription on multiple devices?**
Yes. Your subscription is tied to your account, not a specific device.

**What happens if I cancel?**
You keep access until your current billing period ends, then revert to Free tier. All preferences and library history remain intact.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
Any payment method supported by Apple App Store or Google Play Store (credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay, etc.).

**Can I get a refund?**
Refunds are handled by Apple and Google:
- Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com
- Google: Through Google Play Store (within 48 hours)

Contact brandonqin@berkeley.edu for support with subscription issues.

**What if I exceed my monthly limit?**
You can upgrade to a higher tier or wait until your next billing cycle. The app will notify you when approaching your limit.

- All transactions processed by Apple or Google
- ExtraExtra never sees your payment details
